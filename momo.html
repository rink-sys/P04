About

Momo, a beloved Nepali dumpling, can be made with either non-vegetarian or vegetarian fillings, both of which are widely popular. Non-veg momo typically includes minced meat like chicken, pork, or buffalo, combined with onions, garlic, ginger, and spices. Veg momo is equally favored, filled with finely chopped vegetables such as cabbage, carrots, and sometimes paneer, seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices. It is usually served with a tangy, spicy tomato-based sauce known as "achar".

Ingredients:

Minced meat or mixed vegetables, onions, garlic, ginger, chilies, cilantro, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.

Calories:

400 - 500 calories (varies where you get them from).
